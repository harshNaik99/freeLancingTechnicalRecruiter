The Complete Timeline

M1
Month 1
Foundation & Setup
Establish your legal entity, choose your technical niche, and prepare for launch. This is your investment phase.
Week 1-2: Legal & Financial Setup
Goal: Get legally operational
  • Register business (sole proprietor or LLC)
  • Get EIN from IRS (if US-based)
  • Open business bank account
  • Set up accounting software (Xero or QuickBooks)
  • Create invoice templates
  • Draft basic service agreements/contracts
  • Research tax obligations for self-employment
Cost: $100-500
Week 3-4: Choose Your Technical Niche
Goal: Become a specialist, not a generalist
  • AI/Machine Learning Engineers
  • Cloud/DevOps Engineers (AWS, Azure, GCP)
  • Cybersecurity Specialists
  • Blockchain/Web3 Developers
  • Mobile Engineers (iOS/Android)
  • Data Engineers/Scientists
  • Frontend/Backend Engineers
  • Research salary ranges in your niche
  • Identify 20-30 target companies
  • Study job descriptions to understand requirements
  • Join 5-10 relevant tech communities
  • Follow key influencers in your niche

M2
Month 2
Tech Stack & Online Presence
Build your recruiting infrastructure and establish your professional digital presence.
Week 1-2: Essential Tools Setup
Goal: Build your recruiting infrastructure
  • Choose and set up ATS (Bullhorn, Teamtailor, or Lever)
  • Choose and set up CRM (JobAdder, Recruiterflow, Recruit CRM)
  • Sign up for Juicebox AI (or similar AI sourcing tool)
  • Set up email automation tool (Lemlist, Instantly, Mailshake)
  • Create calendar booking system (Calendly)
Monthly Cost: $200-400
Week 3-4: Build Your Digital Presence
Goal: Look professional and credible
  • Create professional website (WordPress/Squarespace)
  • Add Services, About, Contact pages
  • Include Privacy policy and Terms of service
  • Optimize LinkedIn profile as specialist
  • Professional headshot and banner design
  • Write compelling About section
  • Create content calendar for LinkedIn posts

M3
Month 3
Network Building & First Outreach
Build your candidate pipeline and start engaging with potential clients.
Week 1-2: Build Your Candidate Pipeline
Goal: Have 100+ qualified candidates in database
  • Use Juicebox AI to source 100+ profiles in your niche
  • Connect with 100 candidates on LinkedIn (20/day limit)
  • Send personalized messages to 50 candidates
  • Start building relationships (no hard sells yet)
  • Create a candidate nurture sequence
  • Join tech job boards and communities
Week 3-4: Client Prospecting Begins
Goal: Identify and engage 50 potential clients
  • Connect with 20 hiring managers/CTOs/VPs daily
  • Like and comment on 15 posts daily
  • Post content 3x per week on LinkedIn
  • Send 10 personalized cold emails daily
  • Follow up with previous connections
  • Target startups (Series A-C) and mid-size companies

M4
Month 4
First Placements & Client Acquisition
Close your first clients, execute searches, and make your first placements.
Week 1-2: Close Your First 1-3 Clients
Goal: Sign first contracts

Pricing Strategy:

  • Contingency: 15-20% of first-year salary
  • Retained: $3,000-5,000 upfront + 10-15% on placement
  • Hourly: $60-100/hour for smaller projects
  • Project-based: $2,000-5,000 per hire
  • Send proposals to warm leads
  • Conduct 10+ discovery calls
  • Present 3-5 shortlisted candidates per role
  • Get signed contracts with payment terms
Week 3-4: Execute First Searches
Goal: Deliver value and get testimonials
  • Deep dive into client requirements
  • Source 20-30 candidates per role using AI tools
  • Screen and interview top 10 candidates
  • Present 3-5 qualified candidates to client
  • Coordinate interviews
  • Negotiate offers
  • Support onboarding process

M5
Month 5
Scale & Systematize
Create repeatable systems and expand your client base.
Week 1-2: Refine Your Process
Goal: Create repeatable systems
  • Document your recruitment workflow
  • Create email templates library (50+ templates)
  • Build automated follow-up sequences
  • Set up weekly reporting for clients
  • Create candidate interview scorecards
  • Optimize LinkedIn posting schedule
Week 3-4: Expand Client Base
Goal: Have 3-5 active clients
  • Ask for referrals from happy clients
  • Post success stories on LinkedIn
  • Attend 2-3 virtual networking events
  • Join recruiter communities
  • Consider Upwork/Fiverr for additional leads
  • Double down on cold outreach with proof

AI Automation: Automated sourcing, personalized outreach, interview scheduling, candidate screening

Month 6
Optimization & Growth
Position yourself as a premium specialist and build long-term sustainability.
Week 1-2: Increase Prices & Specialize Further
Goal: Position as premium specialist
  • Raise rates to 20-25% for new clients
  • Create case studies from successful placements
  • Develop specialized assessment processes
  • Build relationships with passive candidates
  • Create a referral program for candidates
Week 3-4: Think Long-term
Goal: Build sustainable business model
  • Set up retainer agreements with top clients
  • Create content that positions you as expert
  • Start building an email newsletter
  • Consider niche job board or community
  • Plan to hire VA or junior recruiter support
